ABOUT US

Ciao, amici! Welcome to Il Caffé!
Our small cafe opened in 2008, when Giuseppe Bombara decided to teach Danes how to drink coffee the Italian way - which is drinking an espresso after the meal. He got inspired by the Italians he met in Aarhus and wanted to bring a piece of Italy in the heart of Aarhus. Il Caffe is the place where you can enjoy a decent cup of espresso, have a delicious brunch or buy something 100% Italian from the shop.
